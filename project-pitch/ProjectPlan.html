Project Information

  • What is the goals/topic of the web app?
    Forward Future is a company that redistributes & resells a collection of curated futuristic fashion and tech.
  • Who is your customer?
    Customers range from the everyday consumer, who register an account with the website.
  • What do users contribute to the database?
    Users contribute Login Credentials, as well as lists of fashion.User contributions may include information such as most popular items, and highest rated items. Users are also able to create wishlists and customize products.
  • What can viewers of the site do?
    Viewers can use browse the site, but will not have access to purchasing and such without creating an account. All registered Users will be able buy, rate, possibly sell fashion tech.
  • What are the relationships in the data-base tables?
    Admins will have access to site statistics, and will be able to update inventory as seen fit. Regular users can shop, rate, comment on fashion tech. Unregistered viewers can can browse the site, but will have limited interactivity with the products. The users will have a username and login, and will be assigned an user ID which will act as the primary key. There may be other tables such as wishlist data that will use the unique user IDs.
